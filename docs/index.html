I have received my Ph.D. in engineering degree from Kyushu University in 2016. My research focuses are:

  1. Environmental Economics
  2. Energy Economics
  3. Geoeconomics
  4. Sustainability Science

I am experienced in econometric analysis, statistical analysis and spatial analysis. I have extensive skills to conduct experimental research by using the methods of online experiment, field experiment and laboratory experiment. If you are interested in my background and consider to collaborate for research, please feel free to contact me.
